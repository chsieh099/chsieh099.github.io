Keela Web Design

As a Design Committee Member in UMA, I was hired to design Networks for Change's website for their new product Keela, a social networking platform for nonprofit organizations.

I started by sketching out the key features of the company and their product.

I then chose colors and created icons that complemented the company's existing brand. I crafted a landing page with a clear navigation bar and the key components for logging in and registering in the top righthand corner.
